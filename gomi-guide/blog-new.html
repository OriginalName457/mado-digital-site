Blog studio

Write a post here, then export the files and upload them. This page is just a tool. It is not linked anywhere public and is marked noindex.

To publish a finished post:
  1. Download the post .html and upload it into the /gomi-guide/blog/ folder on Porkbun.
  2. Download the updated posts.json and upload it to /gomi-guide/blog/, replacing the old one.
  3. That is it. The new post appears on the blog and on the Gomi Guide page automatically.
